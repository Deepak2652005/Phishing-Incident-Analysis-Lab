# Email Indicators

## Suspicious Indicators Identified

* Spoofed sender domain
* Missing DMARC policy
* SPF authentication failure
* Invalid MX records
* Suspicious DNS behavior
* Urgent social engineering language

---

# Phishing Characteristics

* Brand impersonation
* Credential harvesting attempt
* Trust exploitation
* Fake account verification request
* Psychological manipulation tactics

---

# Risk Assessment

| Indicator          | Risk Level |
| ------------------ | ---------- |
| Domain Spoofing    | High       |
| SPF Failure        | High       |
| Missing DMARC      | High       |
| Social Engineering | Medium     |
