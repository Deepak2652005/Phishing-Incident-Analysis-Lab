# Executive Summary

A phishing email impersonating PayPal services was investigated to identify malicious indicators and evaluate potential security risks.

The investigation revealed:

* Domain spoofing activity
* Missing DMARC protections
* SPF authentication failures
* Suspicious infrastructure indicators

The phishing campaign demonstrated common social engineering techniques designed to manipulate user trust and encourage credential theft.

Recommendations include strengthening email authentication controls, improving phishing detection capabilities, and increasing user awareness training.

Overall Risk Level: High
